Summary

This paper focuses on a Zero Trust-based framework that utilizes blockchain and PUF technology for securing the supply chain of Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs). This is useful in demonstrating the value of block chain when information (such as supply chain attestation) can be published publicly as opposed to other use-cases where a public block chain ledger is less appropriate.
