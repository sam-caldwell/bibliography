They report the quantization of the Hall resistance, which occurs at discrete values that are integer multiples of a fundamental constant, highlighting the robustness of this effect against variations in temperature and sample quality. The work emphasizes the significance of the QHE in understanding quantum mechanics and condensed matter physics, as it provides insights into electron interactions and the behavior of electrons in low-dimensional systems. The authors also discuss the implications of their findings for future research, including potential applications in metrology and quantum computing.
