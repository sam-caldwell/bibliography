Star Wars: Episode VI - Return of the Jedi is the third installment of the original Star Wars trilogy, directed by Richard Marquand. As Luke Skywalker continues his training with Yoda, he learns about the dark side of the Force and the importance of confronting his own fears. The film features iconic characters such as Princess Leia, Han Solo, and Darth Vader, culminating in a climactic battle on the forest moon of Endor.
